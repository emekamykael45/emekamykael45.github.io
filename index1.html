About IoD Nigeria Annual Directors' Conference

The IoD Nigeria Annual Directors' Conference is the Institute's flagship event, which is attended by Captains of Industry and Directors in both Private and Public Sectors of the Nigerian economy at which contemporary issues affecting leadership and corporate governance of Nigeria's businesses are x-rayed and global best practice solutions provided by professionals and practitioners in corporate administrations. It is also a forum where board members and management use the once-a-year rare opportunity to meet and network and create business opportunities for their organisations.

The Government Meets Business Dinner and Award Night is an exclusive high-profile event which attracts a cross section of notable Business Leaders and Captains of Industry in both Private and Public Sectors of the economy.
